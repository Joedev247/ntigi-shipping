# Hero Section Videos

Place your shipping/delivery themed videos in this folder.

## Recommended Video Setup

### Option 1: Single MP4 (Simplest)
- Download a video from: Pexels.com, Pixabay.com, or Coverr.co
- Search for: "logistics", "shipping", "delivery", "truck", "cargo"
- Save as: `public/videos/hero-bg.mp4`
- Specs: 1920x1080, H.264 codec, under 5MB

### Option 2: MP4 + WebM (Better Compatibility)
- `public/videos/hero-bg.mp4` - For Chrome, Safari, Edge
- `public/videos/hero-bg.webm` - For Firefox, better compression

## Recommended Free Stock Video Sites

1. **Pexels Videos** - www.pexels.com/videos
   - High quality, free to use
   - Search: "logistics", "shipping", "delivery"

2. **Pixabay Videos** - www.pixabay.com/videos

   - Royalty-free

3. **Coverr** - www.coverr.co
   - Professional, high-quality videos
   - Perfect for hero sections

4. **Mixkit** - mixkit.co/videos
   - Business and movement themed videos
   - Free with no attribution required

## Recommended Video Content

For a shipping app hero, look for videos showing:
- ✅ Trucks/delivery vehicles in motion
- ✅ Packages being loaded/unloaded
- ✅ Logistics hub or warehouse activity
- ✅ GPS/tracking visualization
- ✅ City streets or highways
- ✅ Abstract motion/light patterns
- ✅ Clean, professional atmosphere

## Video Optimization Tips

1. **Duration**: 6-10 seconds for loop videos
2. **Format**: MP4 (H.264) for best compatibility
3. **Resolution**: 1920x1080 or higher (16:9 aspect ratio)
4. **File Size**: Keep under 5MB for fast loading
5. **Codec**: H.264 for MP4, VP9/VP8 for WebM

## How It's Used in the App

The video is displayed as a background in the hero section with:
- 70% opacity green gradient overlay on top
- Decorative pattern overlay for visual interest
- Responsive, covers full viewport
- Falls back to gradient overlay if video fails to load

## Next Steps

1. Download a video from the recommended sites
2. Rename it to `hero-bg.mp4`
3. Save it to `public/videos/`
4. Restart the development server (`npm run dev`)
5. The video will automatically display in the hero section!
